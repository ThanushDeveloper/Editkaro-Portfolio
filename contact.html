Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our process, pricing, and services.

Our standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days for most projects. However, this can vary based on project complexity and scope. Rush jobs (24-48 hours) are available for an additional fee. During our consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your project requirements.

All our packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions depending on the project scope. We provide a detailed revision process where you can submit feedback through our client portal with timestamps and specific notes. Additional revisions beyond the included amount are available at our standard hourly rate.

We deliver videos in multiple formats optimized for your intended platforms. Standard deliverables include MP4 (H.264), MOV, and platform-specific formats for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc. We also provide different resolutions (4K, 1080p, 720p) and aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1) as needed for your distribution strategy.

Project files and raw footage can be provided for an additional fee. This includes the original editing project files (Premiere Pro, After Effects, etc.) and any raw footage or assets used in the project. This is particularly useful for clients who want to make future edits or need the source files for archival purposes.

Our pricing includes video editing, color correction, audio enhancement, motion graphics, and basic visual effects. We also include consultation time, project management, and the specified number of revision rounds. Additional services like custom animations, advanced VFX, or rush delivery are quoted separately based on requirements.

We start every project with a detailed brand guidelines review and style consultation. Our team creates custom color palettes, typography treatments, and motion graphics templates that align with your brand identity. We also maintain strict quality control processes and deliver preview versions for approval before final rendering.
